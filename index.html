I don’t treat finance as reporting—I treat it as decision design. Forecasting and KPI systems help answer: Where is margin created? What breaks at scale? What incentives drive vendor adoption? Which features improve retention vs. cost? That lens shapes what we build and how we price.

How I Approach Data & Finance Problems
